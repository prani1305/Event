Who We Are

JEW – Jayanthi Event World

JEW – Jayanthi Event World is Vizag's most complete event entertainment company. Founded in Visakhapatnam, we've grown into a trusted name across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and major Indian cities — known for delivering events that people genuinely talk about long after the lights go down.

Today, JEW is more than an events agency—it is a robust group of companies and a business empire. Consisting of a diverse portfolio of successful ventures including Best Magician Vizag, Best DJ, and Jayanthi Engineering Works, the JEW umbrella delivers unparalleled excellence across both the entertainment and technical industries.

We are not a generic events agency. We are a team that combines technical expertise, creative entertainment, and genuine warmth to produce experiences that feel personal, polished, and powerful.

Whether it's a child's face lighting up at a magic trick, newlyweds entering on a bed of dry ice fog, or a corporate crowd leaning forward at a product reveal — we live for those moments.

Vasanth Jayanthi

Founder & Event Director

Vasanth Jayanthi is not your typical event manager. He is a man who has spent his career at the intersection of technical excellence and pure entertainment — and that rare combination is exactly what makes JEW different.

A qualified Indian Railway contractor and the founder of Jayanthi Engineering Works, Vasanth brings ISO-standard technical discipline to every event he manages. Logistics don't fail. Equipment doesn't let you down. Timelines are honoured. That engineering mindset is baked into everything JEW does.

But Vasanth's heart has always been on the stage. His deep passion for magic, grand illusions, mimicry, and live performance has shaped JEW's entertainment philosophy from day one. He doesn't just hire entertainers — he understands the craft, the timing, and the psychology of what makes an audience truly captivated.

Today, Vasanth leads a professional team that manages corporate events, product launches, weddings, birthday parties, society celebrations, and DJ shows across Telugu States and beyond. His guiding principle is simple: every guest at every event deserves to leave with a smile.

Vasanth in three words:

Technical. Passionate. Unforgettable.
